About

I have been in the antique brokerage and restoration business for over 30 years, focusing on decor items that span many eras for restaurants and bars. I have helped brands pull together their visual stories - my longest relationship is with Cracker Barrel restaurants - supplying them with well over 12,000 antique framed pictures, as well as sports memorabilia, musical instruments, signs, and countless one of a kind items. Operating within a theme is where I excel, and helping to create that unique atmosphere for guests is what I have been doing for years.
